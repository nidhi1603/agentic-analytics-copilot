# Policy: Operations Escalation

## High Confidence

High confidence explanations require:

- confirmed KPI anomaly
- a dominant shipment or incident pattern
- supporting guidance from metric definitions or runbooks

These cases may include recommended next actions directly in the response.

## Medium Confidence

Medium confidence explanations include partial evidence but still have some uncertainty.

These cases should include likely causes, supporting citations, and suggested follow-up checks.

## Low Confidence

Low confidence explanations occur when:

- no dominant cause appears in the data
- the documents do not support a clear recommendation
- recent changes are not reflected in available evidence

These cases must be marked as `needs analyst review`.
