# Metric Definition: Delivery Success Rate

## Business Definition

Delivery success rate is the percentage of attempted shipments that were successfully delivered for a given region and day.

## Why It Matters

This metric is a core indicator of last-mile execution quality. A sustained drop usually affects customer satisfaction, refund costs, and support volume.

## Common Drivers

- carrier capacity shortages
- route reassignment errors
- address quality issues
- weather-related handoff delays

## First Investigation Steps

1. Check failed delivery counts by failure reason.
2. Review open operational incidents for the same region and day.
3. Compare the metric with on-time delivery performance to determine whether the issue is broader than failed attempts.
4. Review dispatch capacity changes or carrier allocation changes.

## Analyst Guidance

If delivery success rate drops by more than 10 percentage points from the prior day and incident coverage is incomplete, mark the case for analyst review.
