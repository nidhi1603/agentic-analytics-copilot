# Metric Definition: On-Time Delivery Rate

## Business Definition

On-time delivery rate is the percentage of delivered shipments completed within the defined service-level agreement for a given region and day.

## Why It Matters

This metric tracks delivery timeliness, not just delivery completion. It is useful for identifying congestion, routing inefficiencies, and backlog effects.

## Common Drivers

- severe weather
- route congestion
- dispatch backlogs
- handoff delays between hubs and carriers

## First Investigation Steps

1. Compare the metric to delivery success rate to separate delay issues from failure issues.
2. Check weather alerts and transportation incidents.
3. Review average delivery hours and delayed shipment counts.
4. Look for backlog-clearing interventions on the next day.
